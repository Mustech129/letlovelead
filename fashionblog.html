Top Fashion Trends in 2025 You Can’t Ignore

Fashion is always evolving. Here are the trends dominating 2025:

For Men:
  • Oversized shirts & baggy pants.
  • Neutral tones (beige, grey, white).
  • Techwear (functional, modern streetwear).
For Women:
  • Bold jewelry and accessories.
  • Maxi dresses with futuristic prints.
  • Sustainable fabrics (eco-friendly fashion).
For Both:
  • Sneakers are still king.
  • Cross-body bags for style + convenience.
  • Smartwatches as fashion statements.

✅ Final Note: 2025 is all about comfort + style. Mix and match pieces to create your own unique vibe.
