Top 5 Gadgets for Your Home

📺 Smart TV
$300 - $1200

A Smart TV transforms your living room with streaming, apps, and internet access directly on your screen.

    Pros✅

  • ✔️ Endless streaming options
  • ✔️ Voice assistant support

  • ❌ Pricey compared to normal TVs
🥶 Smart Refrigerator
$1000 - $3500

Keeps food fresh while offering touchscreens, shopping lists, and energy-saving features.

  • ✔️ Energy-efficient
  • ✔️ Advanced cooling tech
  • ❌ Expensive to maintain
🤖 Robot Vacuum Cleaner
$200 - $800

Cleans your floors automatically, saving time and keeping your home spotless.

  • ✔️ Hands-free cleaning
  • ✔️ Smart mapping
  • ❌ May miss tight corners
🎵 Smart Speaker
$50 - $300

Plays music, controls smart devices, and answers questions via voice commands.

  • ✔️ Affordable entry-level device
  • ✔️ Works with multiple apps
  • ❌ Privacy concerns
🍲 Microwave Oven
$100 - $500

Heats and cooks food quickly, making it an essential kitchen appliance.

  • ✔️ Saves cooking time
  • ✔️ Compact & efficient
  • ❌ Limited cooking style
