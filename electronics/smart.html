Why Smart Lighting is the Future

Gone are the days of simple bulbs. Smart lighting lets you control brightness, color, and timing — all from your phone.

Benefits of Smart Lighting:
  • Energy Savings: Smart bulbs use 80% less power.
  • Convenience: Turn lights on/off from anywhere.
  • Customization: Set colors to match your mood.
  • Security: Schedule lights when you’re away.
Types of Smart Lighting:
  • Smart Bulbs: Screw into any socket.
  • Smart Strips: Great for decoration.
  • Smart Switches: Control multiple lights at once.

✅ Pro Tip: Start small with a smart bulb kit, then upgrade gradually.
