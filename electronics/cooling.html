Best Cooling Appliances for Hot Nigerian Weather

Nigeria’s heat can be brutal, but the right cooling system makes all the difference.

Options to Consider:
  • Ceiling Fans: Affordable, reliable, low energy.
  • Standing Fans: Portable and effective.
  • Air Conditioners (AC):
    • Split AC: Powerful, good for bedrooms/living rooms.
    • Window AC: Cheaper but noisy.
    • Portable AC: Flexible, no permanent installation.
Energy-Saving Tips:
  • Use inverter ACs — they consume 40% less power.
  • Pair AC with ceiling fans for better air circulation.
  • Maintain filters regularly.

✅ Pro Tip: If your budget is low, invest in a good fan first before moving to AC.
