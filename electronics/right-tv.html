How to Choose the Right TV for Your Home

How to Choose the Right TV for Your Home...
Buying a TV is more confusing than ever. With so many options 4K, OLED, QLED, Smart TVs, how do you know what’s best?

Things to Consider:

1. Screen Size: Bigger isn’t always better. Choose a size that matches your room

  • Small Room: 32" – 43"
  • Medium Room: 50" – 65"
  • Large Room: 70" and above

2. Display Type:

  • LED: Affordable, long-lasting.
  • QLED: Brighter colors, great for daylight rooms.
  • OLED: Best for deep blacks and cinematic quality.

3. Smart Features:

  • Built-in apps (Netflix, YouTube).
  • Voice control (Alexa, Google).
  • Screen mirroring.

4. Budget:

  • Entry-level: ₦80,000 – ₦150,000.
  • Mid-range: ₦200,000 – ₦500,000.
  • Premium: ₦700,000 and above

✅ Pro Tip: If you watch a lot of movies at night, go for OLED. If you watch sports in bright rooms, choose QLED.
